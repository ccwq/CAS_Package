import package refunk.*;

一个时间轴监听类。

用法：

timelineWatcher = new TimelineWatcher(mc);//mc是一个MovieClip

//能发送两种类型（type）的事件

TimelineEvent.LABEL_REACHED=="labelReached" 当到达某有label的关键帧

TimelineEvent.END_REACHED=="endReached"  当到达末尾

timelineWatcher.addEventListener(TimelineEvent.LABEL_REACHED, handleTimelineEvent);
timelineWatcher.addEventListener(TimelineEvent.END_REACHED, handleTimelineEvent);

TimelineEvent有2个属性

currentFrame 接受到事件时候的帧编号
currentLabel 名称
